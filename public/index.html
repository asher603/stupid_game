How to Play

🎮 Controls

WASD / ←↑↓→ — Move

SHIFT — Sprint (uses stamina)

SPACE — Jump

C — Throw a coin behind you

Q / E — Raise / Lower camera

🖱️ LEFT CLICK — Shoot M16 rifle

🎯 Goal

Survive as long as possible! Your score increases over time.
Collect golden coins for +50 bonus points each.

👾 Enemies

Patrick — Chases you directly. Watch out for his dash attack!

Mr. Krabs — Attracted to the nearest coin or you (whichever is closer). Stops briefly after grabbing a coin.

💡 Strategy

Press C to throw a coin behind you to distract Mr. Krabs!
(Costs 1 coin and 50 points)

Use buildings and trees as cover. Manage your stamina wisely — don't sprint all the time!
